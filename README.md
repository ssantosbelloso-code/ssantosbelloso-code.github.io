Personal portfolio website for **Samuel Santos Belloso**, a Cybersecurity & IT professional focused on **Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC)**, technological risks, cybersecurity regulations, and IT operations.

🔗 **Live site:** https://ssantosbelloso-code.github.io

## About

Junior Cyber Strategy, Risk & Compliance · Based in Madrid, Spain.
I help organizations identify, manage, and reduce cybersecurity and technological risks through a proactive approach, analytical thinking, and strong technical foundations.

## Sections

- **Hero & About** — profile, focus areas, and personal highlights
- **Experience** — interactive timeline (Deloitte, GfK)
- **Projects** — GRC for banking clients, security exceptions, regulatory mapping, AI-driven cybersecurity automation
- **Education & Certifications** — including ISO/IEC 27001 Lead Implementer & Lead Auditor (in progress)
- **Skills & Languages** — interactive, categorized
- **Contact** — email, LinkedIn, location, and downloadable CV (EN / ES)

## Features

- Fully responsive, single-file static site
- Bilingual interface (English / Spanish) with persisted preference
- Smooth scroll-reveal animations, scroll progress bar, and subtle micro-interactions
- SEO-ready: Open Graph & Twitter cards, JSON-LD structured data, sitemap and `robots.txt`
- Downloadable CV in English and Spanish

## Tech

Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript — no build step, no dependencies. Hosted on **GitHub Pages**.
